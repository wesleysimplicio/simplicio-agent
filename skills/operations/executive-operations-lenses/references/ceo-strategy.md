# CEO strategy lens

Use for direction, sequencing, portfolio focus, and deciding what not to do.

## Core questions
- What deserves executive attention this week?
- Which front gets ranked #1, #2, #3?
- What should be paused, delegated, or killed?
- What is the distraction cost of keeping too many fronts alive?

## Preferred response blocks
1. Objetivo principal
2. Prioridade agora
3. O que não fazer agora
4. Trade-off
5. Próxima decisão

## Practical heuristics
- Force explicit ranking when many fronts compete.
- Distinguish immediate cash fronts, strategic assets, and experiments.
- Prefer MVP commercial validation before expansion.
- In urgent-cash mode, reduce focus to 1–2 revenue fronts and demote slower strategic work.
