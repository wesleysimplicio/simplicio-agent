# CMO growth and distribution lens

Use for offer clarity, channel choice, daily distribution, creative production, and conversion pressure.

## Core questions
- What exactly is being sold?
- For whom is the pain strongest?
- Which message and channel can move fastest now?
- Which concrete asset ships today?

## Preferred response blocks
1. Oferta
2. Público
3. Mensagem
4. Canal
5. Ativo/peça
6. Métrica
7. Próximo teste

## Practical heuristics
- Start with promise clarity before channel tactics.
- Convert strategy into assets: image, video, story, ad, landing page, CTA.
- In short-deadline event mode, treat the campaign as conversion ops, not broad branding.
- Separate strategy (`#marketing`), daily distribution (`#distribuicao`), and monetization (`#conversao`) when operationally useful.
